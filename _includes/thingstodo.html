Things to do

This region is considered a National Historic Landmark district for its role in the California Gold Rush and referred to as "the whitewater capital" for its fantastic rafting conditions. Our campground is located on the American River with beach access and calming waters, so please bring your bathing suits, biodegradable sunscreen, sunglasses, sandals and beach towels!

Coloma-Lotus Area Things To Do

River Rafting

Our wedding venue, Earth Trek Expeditions, used to also be a river rafting outfitter. Now they have their neighbor Beyond Limits Rafting organize the trips. If interested, let us know ASAP and we can organize group trips.

Beyond Limits Rafting

Sutter's Mill and CA gold discovery area

One of the main attractions of Lotus CA, you can walk around where gold was struck launching the California gold rush in the 1840s. It's easy to get to Sutters Mill and discovery area. There's even still gold in them there hills and you can pan for it!

Sutter's Mill and CA gold discovery area

Lots of beautiful river and foothill hikes, runs, and walks in the area. Most are a short walk or drive away. More from Coloma-Lotus Business Council about it.

Food and Drink in the Area

Please plan on dining out after you check-in on Friday evening. There are several restaurants and pubs located in very close proximity to the venue. We will be providing breakfast and then dinner on Saturday. Please arrange your own lunches on Saturday. We will also be providing breakfast before for those departing on Sunday. There will be snacks, water, non-alcoholic and alcoholic beverages available throughout the three days. We aim to cater to different dietary needs and restrictions, including to those who eat vegan, gluten-free, and nut-free foods. Please find below a map and recommended local restaurants (courtesy of Earthtrek Expeditions).

Squally's On the River

Located right next door to our campground, Squally's On the River is a restaurant serving beers, pizza, BBQ, burgers, and salads. Many fresh ingredients are sourced from local farms, ranches, orchards, breweries, and vineyards.

Squally's On the River

Marcos Cafe

Located just a short walk, Marcos Cafe is an outdoor family-style restaurant serving unique pizzas, salads, fresh appetizers, and local beer and wine. Local live music can also be enjoyed on weekends.

Marcos Cafe

Sierra Rizing Cafe and Bakery

Located in the same parking lot as our campground, Sierra Rizing Cafe and Bakery, is favorite amongst locals and travelers alike. Enjoy a variety of coffee and tea beverages as well as delicious home-baked goods.

Sierra Rizing Cafe and Bakery

Riyo Yogurt

Located in the same parking lot as our campground, Riyo Yogurt, is a self-serve frozen yogurt with delicious flavors (dairy-free as well) and toppings bar that is guaranteed you cool you down on a hot summer day.

Riyo Yogurt

The River Shack Deli and Pub

Located about a quarter of a mile from our campground. The River Shack features made-to-order deli sandwiches and a wide variety of beers. The perfect spot to hang out and live life on river time.

The River Shack Deli and Pub

Coloma Club Bar and Restaurant

Located about a quarter of a mile up the road from our campground. The Coloma Club has a full service bar and is a popular hang-out spot for locals and river guides at night.

Coloma Club Bar and Restaurant

Gorilla Rock Taco

Located across the street from our campground, Gorilla Rock Taco provides tacos, burritos, salad bowls, and beers.

Gorilla Rock Taco

For Beer and Wine Lovers

There’s multiple places to visit in the Lotus/Coloma area! Head to Stash Brewing if you want a foothills German Belgian style Micorbrewery.

Stash Brewing

If wine is your thing, check out Gold Hill Vineyard. They offer a wide array of award-winning red and whites including Syrah, Chardonnay, Barbera, Cab Franc, Cab Sav, etc. They also make a variety of traditional beers!

Gold Hill Vineyard

For Spirits Lovers

If distillates are more your thing, check out California Distilled Spirits! It would be a nice change of pace from beer scene. Be sure to make a reservation if you’d like a tour!

California distilled spirits

Or if you're hankering for some goldrush era moonshine, check out G1848 Distillery.

1848 Distillery
