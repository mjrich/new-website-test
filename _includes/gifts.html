Gifts

Your presence is more than enough of a present for us! Thank you for taking the time and traveling to celebrate with us. Should you insist on a gift, we would love to receive a handwritten card. Feel free to bring a bottle of hard liquor to share with everyone as we’ll only provide beer and wine.

Should you want to make a financial gift, we suggest donating to these local causes:



Sacramento-based organizations:

Sacramento Loaves and Fishes provides food, warmth, and a path home for homeless men, women, and children.

Sacramento Loaves and Fishes

WIND Youth Services promotes the safety, shelter and self-determination of all youth experiencing homelessness, including those who are unstably or unsafely housed – regardless of their ethnicity, gender identity, sexual orientation, sobriety status, engagement in the sex trades, and legal history.

WIND Youth Services

Paul Hom Asian Clinic provides free primary care services to the medically uninsured, including translation services for Asian immigrants.

Paul Hom Asian Clinic

American River Parkway Foundation supports the preservation and enjoyment of the American River Parkway by fostering environmental education, stewardship and volunteer opportunities.

American River Parkway Foundation



Washington DC-based organizations:

Martha’s Table supports strong children, strong families, and strong communities by increasing access to quality education programs, healthy food, and community support.

Martha’s Table

Casa Ruby is a multicultural, bilingual, LGBTQ safe space providing food, shelter, and numerous other health-related services.

Casa Ruby

The Anacostia Watershed Society works to protect and restore the Anacostia River and its watershed communities by cleaning the water, recovering the shores, and honoring the heritage.

The Anacostia Watershed Society

The DC Abortion Fund (DCAF) makes grants to people seeking abortion care in the DC area including those who come from out of state.

The DC Abortion Fund (DCAF)
